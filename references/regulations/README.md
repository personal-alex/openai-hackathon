# Regulatory references

Store attributable, jurisdiction-specific primary sources and implementation notes here. Regulatory material changes; preserve provenance and review dates.

## Suggested layout

```text
regulations/
  IL/
    first-child/
      sources.md
      notes.md
  US/
    first-child/
```

## Required metadata for every source

- Title and publisher
- Direct URL or stable identifier
- Jurisdiction and applicable event(s)
- Retrieval date and last verified date
- Effective date, if known
- Short factual summary
- Limits, caveats, and uncertainty

Prefer official government or institutional sources. Do not store credentials, personal data, or unverified claims. The Navigator must present guidance with source provenance and must not present itself as an eligibility authority.
