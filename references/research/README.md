# Research references

Store product, user-research, accessibility, and technical research that informs Life Navigator here.

## Suggested layout

```text
research/
  user-needs/
  accessibility/
  trust-and-explainability/
  llm-evaluation/
  competitor-notes/
```

## Research note template

For each note, include:

- Research question
- Source and date
- Key finding
- Confidence or limitations
- Product implication
- Follow-up question

Keep observed facts distinct from hypotheses. Prefix assumptions with `⚠️ Assumption:` and log decisions with lasting technical impact in `docs/adr/`.
