# Founder UX Discovery — route companion

## Confirmed founder preference

Life Navigator should primarily be something a person **works through over time and marks progress in**, not only a reference they consult when uncertain.

## UX implication (hypothesis)

The route needs an understandable progress model, a clear current focus, and graceful return states. Completion should reinforce agency without turning the product into a generic productivity app or claiming that an administrative/policy outcome is complete.

## Open question

What situation most commonly triggers the first visit: immediately after the event, after initial confusion, or when a concrete task/paperwork request arrives?
