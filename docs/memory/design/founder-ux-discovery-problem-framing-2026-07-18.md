# Founder UX Discovery — agreed problem framing

## Confirmed framing

Life Navigator is not a chatbot and not a generic task list. It is a transparent route builder for people who feel confused and out of control after a major life event.

It must make clear:

- what deserves attention now;
- what comes later;
- what is known;
- what remains conditional;
- why a question changes the route; and
- how an action is grounded in an official source.

## Primary user outcome

Within the first few minutes, the person should be able to say: “Now I know what I should do and what my next steps are.”

## Main UX risk

Showing too much conditional information could recreate paperwork anxiety and overwhelm. The product must show the map honestly without turning unselected branches into an intimidating list.
