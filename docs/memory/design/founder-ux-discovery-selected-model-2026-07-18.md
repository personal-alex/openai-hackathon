# Founder UX Discovery — selected interaction model

## Founder-selected direction

**Guided Route Companion** — by far the preferred interaction model.

## Model

A visible, explorable, validated route is the primary product artifact. A calm companion asks one decision-changing question at a time to refine the route, but does not force a linear wizard sequence or hide the route behind a chat transcript.

## Why it fits

- Restores clarity and control quickly after days of confusion.
- Shows the whole route at a glance, organized by attention now versus later.
- Supports official-source trust, relevance explanations, and context correction.
- Makes changes after new information or changed circumstances legible.
- Fits the deterministic compiler boundary and hackathon scope.
