# Founder UX Discovery — dual anxiety

## Confirmed founder insight

After a few days of confusion, users may simultaneously fear **too many possible actions** and **having already missed an important action**.

## UX implication (hypothesis)

The route must do more than prioritize. It needs a calm catch-up model: show the current attention horizon, distinguish pending/review-needed/complete states, and make uncertainty visible without blaming the user or implying that any deadline, eligibility, or official outcome has been determined.

## Open question

Which reassurance must come first: a clear immediate next action, or an explicit signal that the route will help them check for missed steps?
