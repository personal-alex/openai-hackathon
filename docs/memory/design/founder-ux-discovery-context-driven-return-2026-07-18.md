# Founder UX Discovery — context-driven return

## Confirmed founder priority

The most valuable return-state signal is **route change after the person updates or corrects their situation**, rather than self-marked task progress alone.

## UX implication (hypothesis)

Context editing and a legible before/after route delta are core product interactions, not secondary settings. Progress remains a private orientation aid, but should not become the primary representation of value.

## Open question

What most commonly causes a person to revisit and update their route: a changed circumstance, newly learned information, or completing a step?
