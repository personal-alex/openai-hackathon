# Life Navigator product design context

- Confirmed primary register: product; platform: responsive web.
- Primary audience: people actively planning a life event. First-session outcome: a clear vision of the process roadmap they need to follow.
- Hackathon differentiator: a deterministic state machine that knows the approved steps for a supported event; future work may integrate with government and other systems to fill or perform required steps.
- Brand direction: a virtual buddy; motto `Life doesn't come with instructions. Now it does.` Avoid wizard-like and dense wiki-like flows; create a chat-like structured experience.
