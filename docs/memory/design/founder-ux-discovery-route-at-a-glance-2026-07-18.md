# Founder UX Discovery — route at a glance

## Confirmed founder preference

The strongest first reassurance should be seeing the **whole route at a glance**, rather than being shown only a single next action. The visible route helps the person see that the situation is finite and manageable.

## UX implication (hypothesis)

Reveal a high-level, comprehensible route early, then use decision-changing questions to refine it. Progressive disclosure should protect against overwhelm without hiding the existence or shape of the whole route.

## Trade-off to explore

A route must be complete enough to create orientation, but not so dense that it recreates the paperwork anxiety the product is intended to relieve.
